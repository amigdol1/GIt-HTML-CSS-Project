About Alex

Alex graduated from the University of Oregon in 2011 majoring in Business and Psychology. Shortly after, she landed a job working at a mobile app company in the Client Services Department. While she enjoyed interacting with people and planning out various projects, she was looking to get more involved within the development process. After a couple of years, Alex started working in the QA Department, where she creates tests plans and tests new features. She tries to find as many bugs as possible before any of the clients who use the product do. While she enjoys QA, she has recently taken an interest in becoming more familiar with the work developers do and is attending courses at epicodus to become more technical.
